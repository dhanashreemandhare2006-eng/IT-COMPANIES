ABOUT


Infosys Limited is an Indian multinational corporation and a global leader in next-generation digital services, information technology (IT), and business consulting. As of 2025, it is the second-largest Indian IT company by revenue and maintains a massive global footprint.

Key Company Information (As of 2025)

Founded: July 2, 1981, in Pune, India, with an initial capital of $250. Founders: N.R. Narayana Murthy and six other engineers (Nandan Nilekani, S. Gopalakrishnan, S.D. Shibulal, K. Dinesh, N.S. Raghavan, and Ashok Arora). Headquarters: Bengaluru (Bangalore), Karnataka, India. Financials (FY25): Revenue: US$19.3 billion (₹166,590 crore). Net Income: US$3.2 billion (₹26,750 crore). Market Capitalization: Approximately US$75.8 billion. Workforce: Over 331,000 employees (Infoscions) representing over 129 nationalities. Global Presence: Operations in 59 countries with 292 locations, including 139 development centers

Cotre Services and Specializations

Infosys provides end-to-end business solutions leveraging its "Global Delivery Model". Digital Transformation: Helping enterprises navigate digital shifts using cloud (Infosys Cobalt) and AI (Infosys Topaz). IT Services: Application development, maintenance, engineering services, and independent validation. Consulting: Management consulting, strategy, and business process management (BPM). Key Platforms: Finacle: A global universal banking solution used in 100+ countries. Panaya: Cloud-based application delivery and testing solutions. Aster: AI-powered platform for marketing efficiency (launched 2024).

Stock Market Listings

Infosys is a publicly-traded company listed on major global exchanges: India: National Stock Exchange (NSE) and Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE) under the ticker INFY. USA: Listed on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) as American Depositary Receipts (ADRs) under the ticker INFY.

Historical Milestones

1993: Initial Public Offering (IPO) in India. 1999: Became the first Indian company to be listed on NASDAQ. 2021: Became the fourth Indian company to reach a $100 billion market cap. 2025: Recognized as a "Global Top Employer 2025" in 20 countries.
