Key Features & Function

Operating System: Runs on Apple's proprietary iOS, known for its user-friendly interface. Hardware: Features high-resolution touchscreens (often OLED/ProMotion), powerful Apple silicon chips (e.g., A-series), advanced camera systems (multiple lenses, computational photography), and Ceramic Shield durability. Company Information

1.Founded: 1981 in Pune, India, by seven engineers. 2.Headquarters: Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. 3.Current CEO & MD: Salil Parekh. 4.Revenue (FY25): US$19.3 billion. 5.Employees: Over 331,000 ("Infoscions") globally. 6.Global Presence: Operations and development centers in over 50 countries. 7.Stock Listings: Listed on the BSE and NSE in India, and the NYSE in the US.

Industries Served:

Infosys provides solutions across a diverse range of industries, including Financial Services, Insurance, Manufacturing, Retail, Healthcare, Communication Services, Energy & Utilities, and Public Sector organizations.

Key Business Philosophy:

The company operates on core values of Client value, Leadership by example, Integrity & transparency, Fairness, and Excellence. Its stated purpose is to "amplify human potential and create the next opportunity for people, businesses and communities" through the effective use of technology.
