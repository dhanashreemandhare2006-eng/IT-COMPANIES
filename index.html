INFOSYS




Infosys is a global leader in digital services, consulting, and IT, founded in India in 1981 by seven engineers, including N.R. Narayana Murthy, growing from a small startup to a multinational tech giant with over 330,000 employees, known for its ethical practices, innovation in AI/cloud, and pioneering the Global Delivery Model for Indian IT. Headquartered in Bengaluru, it provides tech solutions across 59 countries, focusing on digital transformation, talent development, and sustainability.
